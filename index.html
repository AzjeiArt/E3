CryptoArt Printed Art

The BENGAL FLORICAN is a creation that took over 2 decades to be completed, the original was created on parchment with homemade crayon, before it was finished, it was stolen, damaged, recovered, and then lost. It was too badly damaged to restore so it was scanned, its recreation was attempted many times until a small app had the right tools and feel to create it. The Bengal Florican is a near extinct bird native to Cambodia and Vietnam, the artist learned of the bird through two people who served on opposite sides of the vietnam war and had dedicated their lives to making people aware of its struggle to survive.
